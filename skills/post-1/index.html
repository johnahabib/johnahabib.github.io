Skills assignment: making a visual essay

The general concept is to use photoshop, sound editing/production, and video editing to make a visual essay in the same vein as a nerdwriter1 essay about a specific topic. Using audacity to properly record the audio, learning to properly edit it with the software to make it clear and focused, I will overlay the audio with imagery and visual aids made through photoshop. These images will be edited to sync with the audio through adobe premiere, the video editing software commonly used for video post-production.
